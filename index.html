Ongoing Project

A feasibility project is being conducted to assess the use of a WhatsApp-based automated chatbot to support community-driven dengue prevention. The project focuses on enabling residents to identify and eliminate potential mosquito breeding sites within their homes through guided instructions and photo-based reporting.

Participants receive step-by-step guidance via WhatsApp, including short educational content and prompts to inspect key areas of their homes. Residents are then encouraged to submit photos of potential breeding sites, which are reviewed using a standardised assessment approach. This allows for rapid identification of risk and supports timely feedback and action.

The project aims to evaluate the practicality, user acceptability, and operational performance of this digital approach in a real-world urban setting. Key outcomes include participation rates, completeness of submissions, response timeliness, and the feasibility of integrating such tools into routine public health practice.
